About Me

Gastavas Tom Orucho, with over 15 years of passionate dedication to poultry farming, has perfected the art of supplying farm-fresh eggs and chickens directly to consumers and local markets. His unwavering commitment to quality ensures that each product meets the highest standards of freshness and taste, providing a superior dining experience. Tom's expertise extends to creating customized feeding plans tailored to optimize bird health and product quality, resulting in eggs and meat that are both nutritious and flavorful.

Tom's passion for poultry farming is evident in the range of services he offers, highlighting his dedication to education and ethical practices. He provides educational farm visits and tours, giving schools and interested groups valuable insights into sustainable poultry farming. His consultation services offer expert advice on poultry management, helping other farmers maximize efficiency and productivity. Through an online platform, Tom ensures easy access to his premium poultry products with convenient sales and delivery options. Regular health and welfare checks for his birds reflect his commitment to ethical farming, prioritizing their well-being and ensuring the highest standards of care.
